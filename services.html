FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What quality of source photos do you need from me? +
We can work with phone photos, but the better your source — the better the result. Minimum recommended: 800×800 px, good lighting, product clearly visible. Ideally 3 angles per product. No blurry, dark, or heavily compressed images.
How long does delivery take? +
Standard turnaround is up to 48 hours after payment confirmation. In most cases we deliver faster. For large orders (150+ images) we'll confirm the timeline personally.
What formats and resolutions do you deliver? +
We deliver JPG and PNG files in the resolution you select: 1024×1024, 768×768, or 512×512. All files are Shopify and Amazon ready. Commercial rights are included in every pack.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept crypto payments: USDC, USDT, TON, BTC. Payment is processed automatically via OxaPay. Order activates immediately after blockchain confirmation — no manual steps required.
Is my product data kept private? +
Yes. All generation happens on our own private GPU servers — not cloud APIs, not third-party services. Your product photos are never shared with external AI platforms or training datasets. Your data stays yours, always.
What makes Dord AI different from other AI photo services? +
Most services are just wrappers around the same public APIs. We run our own dedicated GPU infrastructure with Flux-based models, and every single order gets human review and manual refinement by our operator. You get bespoke, retail-ready results — not mass-produced output from a shared queue.
